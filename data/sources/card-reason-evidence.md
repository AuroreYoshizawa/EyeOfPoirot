## StatsBomb reconciliation

`yes` means the card is linked to a StatsBomb `Foul Committed` event; `no` means it appears only as `Bad Behaviour`; `unmatched` means no StatsBomb card could be matched to the FIFA card. The complete Bad Behaviour accounting combines the matched `no` table with the published aggregate outside-census count. StatsBomb is a reconciliation source, not the written reason source.
