# Work plan — card-reason audit & expanded-cohort suspension exposure

Logged 2026-07-14. Executor: Codex (data collection and plumbing). Review:
analyst + Claude. This plan is a roadmap, not a methodology amendment: no
new metric may be computed until its formulas are frozen in
`docs/AMENDMENTS.md` (see Task 3 gate). 2026 recomputation waits for the
M101–M104 ingestion already prespecified.

## Task 1 — card-reason evidence audit (grunt work, start immediately)

Goal: classify every in-play player card of 2014/2018/2022/2026 as
`foul_play`, `dissent`, `time_wasting`, `other_nonfoul`, or `unknown`, with
evidence, so that reason-stripped variants of the timing-displacement check
and of suspension exposure can be computed under the prespecified
benign-channel clause.

- Priority P1: all knockout (non-third-place) cards, four editions;
  P2: group-stage cards (needed for Task 3's cohort).
- Evidence protocol (mirrors §4): FIFA documents > federation/club
  statements > established minute-by-minute reports (BBC, Guardian, ESPN,
  Reuters, L'Équipe) > FotMob/Wikipedia/Transfermarkt as documented
  fallback. Every non-`foul_play` classification requires a source URL,
  tier, and a quotation-free paraphrase. If sources do not state a reason,
  the class is `unknown` — never inferred from context or memory.
- StatsBomb open-data foul-linkage (2018/2022) is recorded as a
  corroboration column; disagreements between StatsBomb linkage and the
  written record are flagged `AUDIT`, not silently merged.
- Outputs: `data/derived/source/card_reasons.csv`
  (`card_id, reason_class, sb_foul_linked, source_url, source_tier, note`),
  evidence log `data/sources/card-reason-evidence.md`, raw page snapshots
  under the private archive. Acceptance: 100% of P1 cards classified or
  explicitly `unknown`; a reconciliation table against the StatsBomb
  Bad-Behaviour list; DICTIONARY and manifest updated.

## Task 2 — availability expansion for the widened cohort (complete)

The implementation audit found that this expansion was already present in
the normalized build. All 783 carded players have a complete player × team
schedule grid (3,306 rows) and a computed ω, including 320 players from
group-stage-eliminated teams; edition-team coverage is 32/32/32/48. All 57
served suspension rows have an official absent-lineup observation, and all 56
positive injury/illness intervals have a URL and evidence note. There is no
current unexplained absence; the tested no-evidence path remains
`unexplained`, subtracts no injury minutes, and stays in the denominator.
CI now asserts the full grid, served-suspension provenance, ω formula/bounds,
and the no-evidence denominator behavior.

## Task 3 — expanded-cohort metrics (GATED: freeze formulas first)

Draft design to be finalized as an AMENDMENTS entry before any number is
produced. Rationale (analyst): strong teams coast in the group stage while
weak teams must spend everything, and the knockout-only cohort selects on
survival; group-stage exposure is therefore informative for the full field.

- `E_s^grp`: group-stage suspension exposure, priced precisely with the
  frozen v0.2.1 machinery (minute-granular stop rule, exact-clock
  stoppage-receipt remainders) but with the accumulation horizon capped at
  the group stage's own end for **all** editions, so the group metric
  measures group-stage burden only; the 2014–2022 carry-in channel remains
  exclusively in the primary knockout `E_s` (no double counting).
- `E_s^all = E_s^grp + E_s`, cohort = every team (32/32/32/48), per-foul
  with the existing all-tournament denominator (third place excluded).
- `E_s'` (the exact-clock +0.5 in-match variant, disclosed 2026-07-14 as
  collinear on the knockout cohort) is recomputed on the widened cohort as
  a labeled secondary only.
- Reason-stripped variants (excluding `dissent`/`time_wasting` cards per
  Task 1) of `E_s^all`, of the primary `E_s`, and of the prespecified
  card-timing displacement check.
- `e_s^{md2}` (added 2026-07-14, analyst): for teams reaching the second
  knockout round, widen the scope to knockout matches plus group matchday 2
  only — MD2 is typically played at full effort, while MD1 is exploratory
  and MD3 is often rotated or dead — priced as **in-match impact only** at
  a 1.5× deterrence-minute multiplier (tunable), with no accumulation
  windows; exact formula to be frozen in the amendment.
- Cumulative-fouls-before-card table (added 2026-07-14, analyst): for every
  team-match, the running foul count at the moment of each card (and
  fouls-before-first-card), enabling leniency-threshold comparisons and
  baseline-relative timing rates. The rising within-match carding gradient
  is expected (fatigue → bigger challenges, collapsing defensive shape) and
  is treated as the baseline, not an anomaly.
- Referee-conditioned, opponent-mirror, and comparable-challenge variants
  of the timing checks (candidates from the 2026-07-14 discussion) are
  drafted alongside the amendment.
- Confirmatory questions once M101–M104 and Task 1 land: the magnitude of
  the gradient correlation, and whether Argentina remains the only team
  whose card-timing distribution is anomalous under the frozen checks.
- Discipline: per-edition, never pooled; parameters and any new tunable
  frozen in the amendment; 2026 computed only after M104; results enter
  RESULTS.md as clearly labeled expanded-cohort tables, not replacements
  of the primary registered quantities.

## Sequencing

1. Task 1 P1 → reconciliation report → review checkpoint.
2. Task 1 P2 ∥ Task 2.
3. Amendment freezing Task 3 formulas (analyst approves wording).
4. Task 3 computation, deterministic build, DICTIONARY/CI extensions,
